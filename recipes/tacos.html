Traditional Mexican Street Tacos

Description

These street tacos are easy to make, paced with flavor, and look great too!

These little dynamites are tasty as an appetizer or quick meal.

Ingredients

  • 6 (5-inch) corn tortillas
  • 3 cups chopped cooked chicken
  • 4 sprigs fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped white onion
  • 1 cup guacamole
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Steps

  1. Place a paper towel on a microwave-safe plate and top with tortillas; heat in the
    microwave for 10 seconds.
  2. Place chicken in a microwave-safe bowl; heat in the microwave until heated
    through, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  3. Layer chicken, cilantro, onion, and guacamole, in that order, onto each tortilla.
    Squeeze lime juice over each taco.
